Women Empowerment in India

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Launched: January 22, 2015

This government initiative aims to address gender bias and promote education and welfare for the girl child across India.

Women in the Armed Forces

Milestone: 2020 Onwards

India started granting permanent commission to women in the armed forces and allowed them in combat roles such as fighter pilots in the Air Force.

Self Help Groups (SHGs)

Ongoing

Millions of women across India participate in SHGs that promote financial independence and entrepreneurship at the grassroots level.
